what is this website?

an interactive tool for users to figure out whether they can recycle their item in the city of santa clara.

features

  • tips on recycling based on commonly made mistakes
  • recyclable/not recyclable questionnaire

web app navigation

to use this website, users will see a question at the top of the page, asking them to select their possibly recyclable item from the list of buttons. when a button is pressed, additional information about how to recycle that specific item will be displayed.

if the item is not one of the buttons, the user is told that their item is not recyclable. below this section, the sources referenced for recycling instructions are displayed.
